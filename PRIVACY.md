# Privacy Notice

**Last Updated:** January 27, 2026

## Privacy at a Glance

The Enterprise Support app is designed with privacy as a core principle:

- ✅ **No Personal Data Collection** - We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- ✅ **Local Storage Only** - All data stays on your device
- ✅ **No Tracking** - No analytics, no third-party services
- ✅ **No Account Required** - Use the app without registration or login

## What This Means for You

- Your privacy is protected by design
- No data is sent to external servers
- Support documents are stored locally on your device
- You control your data by controlling your device

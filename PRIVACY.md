# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 28, 2024

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Enterprise Support mobile application ("the App") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information Collection and Use

### No Personal Data Collection

The Enterprise Support app is designed with privacy as a priority:

- **No Account Required:** The app does not require user registration or login
- **No Personal Information:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- **No Tracking:** We do not track user behavior or analytics
- **No Third-Party Services:** The app does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising services

### Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

- **Support Documents:** IT support documents and help content are stored locally for offline access
- **User Preferences:** App settings and preferences are stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Sync:** No data is synchronized to cloud services or external servers

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms
- Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
- No data is transmitted over the network after initial app installation

## Changes to Documents

The app may download updated support documents from your organization's configured repository. This is limited to:

- Reading publicly available support documents
- Downloading updates when available
- No personal information is sent during these updates

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for enterprise use by employees.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All app data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by:

- Deleting the app from your device
- Clearing app data through iOS Settings

## Compliance

This app is designed to comply with:

- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Other applicable privacy laws

Since no personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers, the app minimizes privacy risks.
